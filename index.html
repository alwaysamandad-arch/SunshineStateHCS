About Sunshine State HCS

Sunshine State Home & Commercial Services was built on a simple belief: every homeowner deserves honest, high-quality work at a fair price. We’re an owner-led team based in Jacksonville, FL, and we treat every property — whether a single-family home or a commercial space — with the same care we’d give our own.

Our crew includes Kevin, our water damage and restoration specialist, and Rob, who leads our electrical, painting, and kitchen projects. When you work with us, you’re getting skilled tradespeople who take pride in their craft — not just a day’s work.
